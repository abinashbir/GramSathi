Empowering Communities with Sustainable Water Solutions

A low-cost, user-friendly digital tool for Gram Panchayats to monitor, manage, and sustain rural piped water supply systems under the Jal Jeevan Mission.

Discover the Solution

Our Core Features

Easy Monitoring

Track infrastructure performance, pump running hours, and valve status through a simple, intuitive dashboard.

Early Fault Detection

Leverage predictive insights from operational data to identify potential leakages and system failures before they happen.

Water Quality Tracking

Record and access results from portable digital testing kits to ensure a consistent supply of safe drinking water.

Community Empowerment

Equip VWSCs with the data they need for local decision-making, promoting ownership and accountability.

Expected Outcomes

  • Improved Reliability

    Village functionaries can perform routine checks, leading to fewer breakdowns and more reliable water service.

  • Reduced System Failures

    Timely maintenance based on predictive data minimizes costly repairs and system downtime.

  • Assured Water Safety

    Regular, digitally recorded quality tests ensure that the water reaching households is safe for consumption.

  • Greater Local Ownership

    Gram Panchayats are empowered to manage their water assets independently, reducing reliance on external agencies.
